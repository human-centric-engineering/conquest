# F14.6 — Cohort Report versioning, publish, themed PDF & search

> Committable tracker for **F14.6**, the final P14 feature. Revision history + restore, a
> draft/published lifecycle, a branded downloadable PDF (any revision), and cross-report search —
> the operational surface around the generated/edited report.

## Intent

Once a cohort report exists and is editable, admins need to manage its lifecycle: review and restore
past iterations, publish a chosen revision, download a themed PDF at any point, and find a published
report later. F14.6 adds that surface on top of the append-only revision model from F14.3.

## Decisions

- **Restore = append, never rewrite** — restoring a past revision appends its content as a new
  `admin` revision, preserving the full history; the working head stays the highest number.
- **Publish pins a revision** — `publishedRevisionNumber` on the report; publish defaults to the head,
  unpublish clears it. The PDF can target `head` | `published` | a specific number.
- **PDF: text + bar charts, themed by accent + logo** — `@react-pdf/renderer` can't render HTML, so
  section bodies are flattened to text paragraphs (`htmlToParagraphs`) and charts are drawn as
  labelled bars from the shared `ChartData` (no recharts in PDF). Branding via `resolveTheme`
  (demo-client accent + best-effort logo prefetch to a data URI, so render does no network I/O).
  Available for any revision so a draft is downloadable "during the process".
- **Search = published-only, title + section text** — a pragmatic in-memory filter over published
  reports (optionally scoped to a demo client) with snippets; within-report find is the browser's
  native search. (A stored tsvector is a later optimisation if volume grows.)

## What shipped

- **Schema** — `AppCohortReport.publishedRevisionNumber`. Migration `…_app_cohort_report_publish`
  (create-only, phantom pgvector DDL stripped, applied via `migrate deploy`).
- **Persist** — `listCohortReportRevisions`, `restoreCohortReportRevision`, `setCohortReportPublish`,
  `getCohortReportRevisionContent` (resolves head/published/number); view carries
  `publishedRevisionNumber`.
- **PDF** — `pdf-model.ts` (`htmlToParagraphs` + `buildCohortReportPdfModel`, pure),
  `CohortReportPdfDocument` (react-pdf), `render-cohort-report-pdf.tsx` (logo prefetch + renderToBuffer).
- **API** — `GET …/export.pdf`, `POST/DELETE …/publish`, `GET/POST …/revisions` (list/restore),
  `GET /api/v1/app/cohort-reports/search`. Endpoint helpers added.
- **UI** — round panel gains Publish/Unpublish, Download PDF, and a History list with Restore;
  shows which revision is live.
